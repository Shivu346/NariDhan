About NARIDHAN

Empowering rural women through financial literacy and accessible tools

Our Story

NARIDHAN was founded with a vision to bridge the financial literacy gap among rural women in India. We recognized that despite being key contributors to their family's economy, rural women often lack access to financial knowledge and tools that could help them secure their future.

Our journey began in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, where we witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by rural women in managing their finances. We saw how limited access to banking services, lack of financial education, and absence of proper guidance were hindering their economic growth.

This inspired us to create a comprehensive platform that not only provides financial education but also offers practical tools and personalized guidance to help rural women take control of their financial future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower rural women with financial knowledge, tools, and resources that enable them to make informed financial decisions, achieve economic independence, and secure their future.

Our Vision

A future where every rural woman in India has equal access to financial services, possesses the knowledge to manage her finances effectively, and has the confidence to pursue economic opportunities.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We believe in empowering women with knowledge and tools that enable them to take control of their financial future.

Accessibility

We are committed to making financial education and tools accessible to all women, regardless of their location or background.

Trust

We build trust through transparency, integrity, and a genuine commitment to the well-being of the women we serve.
